## Overview

Added a set of 4 ecommerce transactional email templates to the Community section of react-email.

## Templates

- **Order Created** — confirms the order was received with expected delivery date and order summary
- **Order Confirmed** — confirms payment with payment method details and order summary
- **Order Shipped** — notifies shipment with tracking number, carrier, estimated delivery, and a track button
- **Order Delivered** — confirms delivery with delivery date, review CTA, and order summary

## Tests

Updated `get-emails-directory-metadata.spec.ts` in both `packages/react-email` and `packages/ui` to include the new ecommerce directory and its 4 templates in the expected directory metadata output.
